## The method

**Section label**

The Momentum Room process

**Headline**

Prepare. Confirm. Protect. Review.

### 1. Prepare the trade

Identify the catalyst, levels, expected pattern, and conditions required before the opening bell.

### 2. Wait for confirmation

Trade the setup only when price and volume confirm the plan. No trigger means no trade.

### 3. Define the risk first

Know where the idea fails before considering the potential reward.

### 4. Review the decision

Study the plan, execution, and outcome so every trade produces a lesson.

**Supporting line**

The goal is not to copy Mason. It is to understand the process well enough to make your own decisions.

## FAQ

### Is this a signal group?

No. Mason discusses stocks he is watching and trades he takes, but every setup includes the market context, confirmation, invalidation, and risk reasoning. You remain responsible for every trading decision.

### Who is the membership for?

The Momentum Room is for developing self-directed stock traders who understand basic orders and charts but need a more repeatable momentum-trading process.

### What if I work during market hours?

Every live session is recorded. A concise recap is posted by 4:30 p.m. ET so you can review the plan, decisions, and lessons after work.

### Which markets do you cover?

The membership focuses on US-listed momentum stocks. It does not cover options, futures, forex, or crypto.

### How much time should I spend inside the Discord?

Start with the premarket plan and same-day recap. Most members can follow that core workflow in 30-45 minutes per trading day. Live participation is optional.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel online from your account before the next renewal date. Your access continues through the paid billing period.

### What is the refund policy?

First-time members may request a full refund within seven calendar days of purchase. Renewals are not refundable.

### Will joining make me profitable?

No. Trading involves substantial risk, and no educator or community can guarantee results. The membership helps you develop a decision process; your results depend on your actions and market conditions.

### Is this financial advice?

No. All content is educational and informational. It does not consider your financial situation, objectives, or risk tolerance.

## What he’s trying to solve

- Replace scattered social-media tips with a repeatable trading process
- Understand why a setup is valid instead of copying unexplained entries and exits
- Get timely market analysis, feedback, and accountability around a full-time job
- Find an active peer community without exposing himself to hype, impersonation, or fraud
- Decide whether recurring Discord access produces enough practical value to justify its cost

## Buying criteria (order)

- **Credibility + transparency**: verifiable identity, relevant experience, realistic claims, disclosed incentives, and visible risk language
- **Concrete Discord value**: published schedule, channel preview, content frequency, moderator access, and clear membership deliverables
- **Education over dependency**: explanations, playbooks, and reviews that help him make independent decisions instead of blindly copying signals
- **Community quality**: active moderation, focused discussion, beginner-safe questions, and controls against spam or impersonation
- **Price flexibility**: transparent monthly price, simple cancellation, and no surprise renewal or upsell
- **Schedule fit**: recordings, recaps, and alerts that remain useful outside market hours

## Pain point statements

- As a **developing retail trader**, I want **a repeatable process for evaluating setups**, so that **I can make decisions without chasing social-media hype**.
- As a **full-time employee who trades part-time**, I want **recorded analysis and concise market recaps**, so that **I can learn without abandoning my work schedule**.
- As a **cautious finfluencer follower**, I want **verifiable credentials, disclosures, and realistic performance context**, so that **I can assess credibility before paying**.
- As a **prospective Discord member**, I want **a preview of current channels, activity, and weekly deliverables**, so that **I can judge whether the subscription provides ongoing value**.
- As a **budget-conscious subscriber**, I want **transparent renewal, cancellation, and refund terms**, so that **I can control the cost of trying the community**.

## How he decides (journey snapshot)

- **Awareness**: discovers the influencer through YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, X, Reddit, or a shared trade breakdown
- **Consideration**: watches several free posts, checks consistency, searches the influencer's background, compares paid communities, and looks for unscripted member feedback
- **Trial**: chooses a monthly plan or limited trial after reviewing a real Discord preview, weekly schedule, and cancellation terms
- **Enrollment**: expects secure checkout, an immediate Discord invitation, role assignment, and a clear start-here path
- **Retention**: stays when teaching remains consistent, discussion stays useful, moderators control noise, and the community improves his decision process

## Channels

- **Discovery**: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, X, Reddit trading communities, podcasts, and creator collaborations
- **Evaluation**: landing page, free long-form videos, Discord previews, third-party reviews, FINRA BrokerCheck, and Investor.gov
- **Comms preference**: Discord for scheduled analysis and community updates; email for receipts, renewal notices, policy changes, and account recovery
